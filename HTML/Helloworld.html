Magic Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 stick of butter

Directions

  1. Spread into a non-stick dish
  2. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  3. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  4. Eat and enjoy!
